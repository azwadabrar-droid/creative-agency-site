FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?
+
Most brand + website projects take 6–10 weeks from kickoff to launch. Enterprise projects vary based on scope. We'll give you a precise timeline after our discovery call.
Do you work with international clients?
+
Absolutely! We work with clients globally. Our async-first workflow means timezone differences are rarely a problem. We use Slack, Notion, and Loom to keep everything smooth.
What do I need to get started?
+
Just book a free discovery call with us. Come with your goals, challenges, and rough budget in mind. We'll handle the strategy from there and put together a tailored proposal.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
+
Yes — every package includes post-launch support. Enterprise clients get dedicated retainer support. We also offer optional monthly maintenance plans for updates, performance monitoring, and content edits.
What technologies do you use?
+
We build with React, Next.js, Webflow, and Shopify for web. For mobile we use React Native and Flutter. All code is clean, documented, and handed over fully to you at launch.
